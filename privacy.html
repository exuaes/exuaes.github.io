1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how an individual ("Operator", "I", "me") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when providing this website ("Service").

This Service complies with:

  • Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) of the Republic of Korea,
  • Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization (Network Act),

This policy applies to all Users ("you") who access or use the Service.

2. Personal Information Collected

The Service collects only the minimum amount of information necessary to operate securely and function properly.

2.1 Automatically collected information

When you visit the Service, your browser and hosting platform may automatically transmit technical data such as:

  • IP address (temporarily visible in hosting logs)
  • Browser type, version, and language
  • Device type, operating system
  • Access time, pages viewed, and referrer information
  • Cookies essential for functionality
  • Basic error logs (for security and debugging)

This information is used solely for maintaining and securing the Service.

I do not use this information to personally identify Users.

2.2 Information you voluntarily provide

The Service does not require sign-up, login, payment, or personal profile creation.

However, you may voluntarily provide:

  • vocabulary data entered manually,
  • imported JSON/CSV/TSV files,
  • text you input into any field,
  • email address if you send me an inquiry at support@ey.io.kr.

2.3 Information NOT collected

This Service does not collect or request:

  • name, phone number, physical address
  • resident registration number
  • payment or transaction data
  • location tracking or GPS information
  • biometrics or sensitive categories (religion, health, politics, etc.)
  • advertising IDs
  • user behavior tracking systems (e.g., Google Analytics, Meta Pixel)

4. LocalStorage and Cookie Usage

4.1 LocalStorage

The Service uses LocalStorage to save:

  • vocabulary lists,
  • search configurations,
  • imported data,
  • user preferences.

Important notes:

  • LocalStorage data is stored only on your own device.
  • I cannot access, view, track, or recover LocalStorage contents.
  • Clearing browser data, switching devices, or using private mode will delete your data.
  • You are responsible for exporting backups via the built-in Export function.

4.2 Cookies

Only essential cookies are used (for styling, language display, or UI behavior).

This Service does not use tracking or marketing cookies.
